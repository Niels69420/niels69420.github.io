In this game you take control of the Fish and Salmon and you can use a variety of moves to deal damage to the enemy.
You can use the Attack button to deal damage to the enemy. This cost no bubbles.
You can use the Special moves button to open a menu. This menu shows you special moves you can use. Special moves costs bubbles which are required to use the special moves. The following special moves are:
- Whirlpool: Deals damage to the enemy and has a chance to stun the enemy. Costs 4 mana.
- Combo attack: Deals a lot of damage to the enemy. Costs 5 bubbles.
- Heal: Heals the player using the move. Costs 4 bubbles.
There is one last move you can use which is the Defend button. This move reduces the damage you take from then ext attack of the enemy. Defending will give you back 3 bubbles, so use it when you are running low.

After the game ends your lowest amount of turns will be saved. You can see the lowest amount of turns for each enemy on the bottom of the screen.
